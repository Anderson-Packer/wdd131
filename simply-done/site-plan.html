Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to give the user a place to make a simple to-do list. To-do lists help people feel organized and provide a sense of accomplishment for completing tasks.

Audience

Anyone who has tasks to complete

Dynamic elements

The site will use dynamic elements to generate the to-do list. Each item will be stored as an object and arrays and array methods will be used to manages the list. There will also be buttons with functions to add, remove, and clear the list. Each item will have a check box that will us DOM manipulation to appeal checked when clicked.

Content

To Page

Click the "add" button and type the item that you would like to add in the box. To remove an item just click the "remove" button, hover over the item you wish to remove and click to remove it. If you are done with your list click the "clear" button.

About Page

Using a to-do list is one of the simplest and most effective ways to stay organized. It helps you clearly see what needs to get done, prioritize important tasks, and avoid forgetting responsibilities throughout the day. A good to-do list reduces stress by breaking work into manageable steps and giving you a sense of progress as you check things off. Whether you're planning a busy day or just keeping track of small errands, a to-do list helps you stay focused, productive, and in control.
